Who we are

A network for the builders of tomorrow

FUTEEN is a global foundation and network for nurturing young futurists in the age of artificial intelligence. We bring together teenagers and young adults so they don't just consume technology and the future — they learn to understand it, think critically about it, and take an active role in shaping it.

The name blends two words — Future and Teen — for the young people who are building what comes next.
